Badges
Badges are small circles, positioned either at top-right or bottom-right of the parent component. Badge can be used to display numbers, online / offline status, depending on where they are used.
Profile Badges
Profile Badge is used to show the current status of a user. It can be used with 3 variants : online | offline | dnd 
                                    
                <div class="badge_Container" >
                    <img class="avatar sm" src="../../assests/Avatar5.jpg" alt="avatar-1">
                    <span class="online"></span>
                </div>

                <div class="badge_Container" >
                    <img class="avatar md" src="./../assests/Avatar6.jpg" alt="avatar-1">
                    <span class="offline"></span>
                </div>

                <div class="badge_Container" >
                    <img class="avatar lg" src="./../assests/Avatarfa-stack-1x.jpg">
                    <span class="dnd"></span>
                </div>
